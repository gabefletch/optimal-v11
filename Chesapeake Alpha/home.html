Changing the landscape of modded software.

Optimal provides direct links to modded Android and iOS apps to avoid sketchy websites and phishing tactics. Completely free, and no jailbreaking necessary.

Optimal is an open source project created by
Gabe Fletcher, with support from Ella Hawkins
and John Fdoul. View our open source library
at dub.sh/optimal-github
